Area Calculator Formulas

Square

Formula: Area = Side × Side

To calculate the area of a square, multiply the length of one of its sides by itself.

Example: If the length is 4 units, the area is 4 × 4 = 16 Sq.

Triangle

Formula: Area = 1/2 × Base × Height

To calculate the area of a triangle, multiply the base by the height and then divide by 2.

Example: If the base is 5 units and the height is 8 units, the area is 1/2 × 5 × 8 = 20 Sq.

Rectangle

Formula: Area = Width × Height

To calculate the area of a rectangle, multiply the width by the height.

Example: If the width is 6 units and the height is 7 units, the area is 6 × 7 = 42 Sq.
